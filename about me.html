Hi, I am from Jodhpur city in the state of Rajasthan, India. I received my B.Tech. degree in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, India in 2012. Although I am an electrical engineer by training, I have always been interested in studying natural physical phenomena, and the workings of brain. I am really fascinated by the idea that computing and thinking can arise just using physical laws of nature. Hence, in order to explore the world of computing, I am currently working towards my Ph.D. degree at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, where I am researching on building continuous time dynamical systems using circuits which have the capability to compute. I am working with my advisor, Prof. Arijit Raychowdhury, in the ICSRL Lab at Georgia Tech.
